Chicken Stir Fry

Description

A quick and healthy Asian-inspired dish featuring tender chicken and colorful vegetables in a savory sauce. Perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Stir-fry chicken until golden brown.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add garlic, ginger, and soy sauce.
  6. Cook for 2 more minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  7. Serve hot over rice.
